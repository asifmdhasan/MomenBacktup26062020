Animated Motion

It's really exciting to watch pixels and coordinates of your canvas acting your way. I find animation as an excellent method of story-telling by inanimate objects. This method is very useful particularly in teaching and learning. To be very exact - life is nothing if it does'nt move!

I never tried to animate with utmost sincerity, rather I have been translating some of my story-telling necessities into sort of animations with the help of my favourite tools depending on time and taste.

While creating 'Aaj Amader CHHUTI' during late nineties and early this century, it was necessary to create small animations to introduce alphabets to toddlers. In that course I ventured to probably beyond my limit! As a naive and amateur practitioner of animation, I could only produce a few like this.
