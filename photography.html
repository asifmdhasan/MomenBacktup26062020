My Passion

Pictorial beauty of the nature attracts me the most since the time I have started to feel the presence of so many captivating elements around. I am a nature lover. As such, I never forgot to take my cameras and lenses along before setting out on a journey. I have always been trying to capture the nuances of the nature whenever an opportunity comes.

Figures come into my frame as part of the nature. I always try to discover the wonderful and amazing impacts made by the forms of elements, arcs of trees, outlines of high cliffs, peeping sunshine through thick leaves and the interesting combination of lights and shades.

As part of my professional responsibility I travelled to a number of places of some part of the globe. Being an amateur photographer and a photo enthusiast, I tried to capture some impressive and immersive moments of the nature including some places of name and fame. I wish to accumulate all those moments and places in the form of publications and photo albums whenever I get a chance.

My photographs and digital creations have been exhibited in a number of group shows and photo salons held in a few countries including Japan, Romania, India and Bangladesh. Apart from these group shows I held a solo photo exhibition entitled "Far from the madding crowd" in Kolkata, India in June 2009. I also achieved a few international recognitions in photography and digital imagery including Federation of Asian Photographic Art (FAPA) Award.
