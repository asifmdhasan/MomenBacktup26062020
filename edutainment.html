EDUTAINMENT

learning in playing, invoking insight
Applying thoughts in learning

The time was the early days of digital era in Bangladesh. Desktop computers have just started to occupy the table-tops of affluent people in Dhaka city but their usage was limited to only word processing. I, along with two of my friends, took the challenge of producing something different for the tender-aged modern children of time.

Being aware that the conventional method of educating children is too formal, clich√© and infrastructure oriented, we started to design and develop a computer based multimedia tool for children particularly for those who could be impressed upon by music, animation and visual aid to the learning; not in a formal way but through playing and making fun. Since the object was to educate through entertainment, we termed it EDUTAINMENT!

Starting from 1999, it took almost three years to complete the program which has been named 'Aaj Amader CHHUTI'. These three Bangla words translate as 'It's Our Holiday Today'. It's an interactive bilingual multimedia tool in vernacular Bangla and in English language. This tool has got some features interesting for all but we considered it to be most appropriate for children aged below 14 years.

I led a team of three content creators and developers for long three years to develop 'Aaj Amader CHHUTI'. Other members were Khaled Asif and Shamaun Shafqat. focusfutureWorks and MOHONA, two Dhaka based firms were kind enough to provide us all the logistic, technical and financial support on totally complimentary basis. The tool was formally inaugurated in 2002 at the Bangabandhu International Conference Centre, Dhaka. Renowned academician and researcher Professor Jamilur Reza Chowdhury has kindly launched the Disk in presence of social elites, high dignitaries and technology enthusiasts from all strata of life.

If you are visiting this site on a mobile or tab device such as cell phone, iPad, Galaxy or Lenovo tab etc., I am sorry to mention that the contents of 'Aaj Amader CHHUTI' will not be visible to you because of the lack of browser support for flash content and Flash Player. You'll not even notice existence of any content below on the devices mentioned above. You may be aware that the Adobe Systems have decided to stop updating and distributing Adobe Flash/Flash Player from December 31, 2020. As such, the majority of e-learning content made with Flash and delivered via browser, has to be moved to another platform. We are working on it. But you are open to view the contents of 'Aaj Amader CHHUTI' on any desktop/laptop computers only by enabling 'Flash Player' from inside your browser.
